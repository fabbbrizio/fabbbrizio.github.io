Fabrizio Goglia

Interaction Designer

Hello, I am Fabrizio, interaction designer and random illustrator. I work across different communication disciplines; from identity, user experience and interface design, illustration for digital and print. As designer I had the pleasure to work in team with independent design collective, as Iperstudio and digital agencies. I collaborated as researcher and designer with a program of United Nations Office for Project Services and other organizations. I studied at IUAV, University of Venice, attending the IxD program run by Gillian Crampton Smith and Philip Tabor.
